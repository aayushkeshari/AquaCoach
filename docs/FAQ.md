# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

### ❓ How does AquaCoach calculate my hydration goal?
Your hydration goal is based on your weight, activity level, and weather conditions. If HealthKit is enabled, activity data is also used.

---

### ❓ Is my data private?
Yes. All personal data is stored securely on your device. AquaCoach does not sell or share user data.

---

### ❓ Why didn’t I receive a reminder?
Make sure:
- Notifications are enabled in Settings.
- Do Not Disturb mode is off.
- The app has permission to send alerts.

---

### ❓ Can I change my hydration goal manually?
Currently, goals are automatically calculated, but manual overrides may be added in future updates.

---

### ❓ Does the app work offline?
Yes. Logging works offline. Weather adjustments require internet access.

---

### ❓ What if HealthKit sync fails?
Check:
Settings → Health → AquaCoach → Allow Data Access  
If the issue persists, disable and re-enable Health permissions.

---

### ❓ How do I reset my data?
Go to:
Settings → Reset Data  
⚠ This action is irreversible.
