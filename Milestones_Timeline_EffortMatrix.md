Below are the major planned milestones and deliverables for the Hydration Tracking App project. Each milestone represents a measurable checkpoint in the development cycle.

| **Milestone #** | **Milestone Title** | **Planned Date** | **Description** |
|-----------------|--------------------|------------------|-----------------|
